CrazyBot help!

Commands:

- help | Displays help
- suggest {Idea} | Suggest a new command
- magic8 | {Question} Acts like a magic 8 ball
- gif | Will send you a random gif from a SMALL list
- searchgif {Keyword} | Will send you a random gif that has your keyword in it's name (using Giphy API)
- large {YourText} | Displays 50px text
- huge {Your text} | Displays 150px text
- enormous {YourText} | Displays 300px text
- magictext {YourText} | Displays 100px, italic, bold and underlined text
- math {Calculation} | Does the math for you ! (doesn't take fractions)
- praise | Will praise you for example if you need moral support!

Message extension:

Creates a Hero Card and puts it into your text box.
